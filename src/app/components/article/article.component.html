HCL Technologies gains 5% on better-than-expected Q3 earnings HCL Technologies was up for the sixth straight day, gaining 8 per cent, against 2 per cent decline in the S&P BSE Sensex. Business Standard

Shares of HCL Technologies were up 5 per cent to Rs 1,035 apiece on BSE in the intra-day trade after the company reported a better-than-expected net profit of Rs 2,611 crore for the quarter ended December 2018 (Q3FY19) over the September quarter. Revenue grew 5.6 per cent at Rs 15,699 crore sequentially.
